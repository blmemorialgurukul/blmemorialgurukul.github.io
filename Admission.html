Rules of Admission

  1. Admission is granted to boys and girls irrespective of cast and creed.
  2. Admission is strictly based on entrance exams and personal interviews of the child and their parent.
  3. The registration fee is non-refundable. It does not guarantee admission in any way.
  4. The provided date of birth in the admission form will be considered final and no requests for changing the name or date will be entertained.
  5. On the basis of the candidate's performance in the interview, he/she will be issued an admission order. Within 7 days of the admission order, parents are required to provide the deposit fee allong with any other required documents and photographs.
  6. A student's admission is renewed every year, depending on the student's overall performance.
  7. At the time of admission any transfer students must produce transfer certificates and progress reports from the previous school (excluding nursery school). K.G. prep with the transfer certificate and birth certificate must be provided by the parents.

School Rules

  1. All the students should reach the school at least 5 minutes before the bell rings
  2. Students coming late more than twice in a month will be penalized
  3. The students must carry with them their identity card and school diary. Failing to do so will result in them not being allowed to enter the class
  4. The school uniform is compulsory
  5. Running, playing, or shouting inside the school is prohibited
  6. Any breach of discipline by students may lead his/her suspension and even expulsion.

Attendance

Attendance is required for all students to be eligable to take the final examinations (excluding students who are absent in cases of sickness or emergencies). The total number of classes attended is counted from the date of admission for students. Leave is granted to students once provided a written application from the parents/guardians of the student or on the request noted in the school diary. The signatures of the parents/guardians must match those provided on the admission form card. No student can leave school premises during school hours without seeking permission from the Principal.

School Uniform

All students are required to come to school in their proper school uniform. Parents and/or guardians are asked to manage their uniforms during the year.

Summer Uniform:

  1. Girls -  Blue skirts with yellow shirts, ties with stripes, yellow socks, black leather shoes, yellow ribbons
  2. Boys -  Blue trouser with yellow shirts, tie with stripes, yellow socks, black leather shoes

Winter Uniform:

  1. Girls -  Grey slacks with a grey sweater or blazer
  2. Boys -  Grey slacks with a grey sweater or blazer
