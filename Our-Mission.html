BLMG

Aims and Aspirations

1. The school aims to give a refined education
2. To impart education to all irrespective of caste, religion, and community
3. To incubate sound, moral values and to revitalize ancient values and reintegrate them into the changingneeds of modern times
4. Training the students physically, academically, and morally to met the challenges of the modern world
5. To make schooling a pleasant experience by freeing the child from heavy homework
6. Ensure progress, properity, and welfare of the children
7. To sow the needs of tender love and affection, and to rear rich and positive thoughts

Our Founders

Banwari Lal Memorial Gurukul was founded in 2003 by Late Shri Banwari Lal Ji Yadav and Late Smt. Janki Devi Yadav. Their goal was to provide an equal opportunity education for everyone, no matter what caste or religion. We continue to honor their beliefs, and strive to help every child get an education.

From the Director's Desk

Mrs. Shakuntala Yadav

Banwari Lal Memorial Gurukul (BLMG) is a co-educational English medium school. At BLMG, we believe that education is the key to creating a strong society and we are dedicated to creating, developing, and maintaining a strong academic environment for our students. In this rigorous environment, children can grow and develop into better problem solvers in every facet of life instead of being one-dimensional students. Here at BLMG, we feel responsible in the proper education of our youth, and we wish for your cooperation and involvement to achieve this mission.

Banwari Lal Memorial Gurukul is an English medium academy, and we specialize in providing comprehensive, quality education to develop every child's personality. The educational journey of this school started when it was a small primary school, and it has since grown into a secondary school. This school, due to your unbreakable faith, has reached near the top as we provide high quality education in modern facilities. The main aim of BLMG is to harness the hidden potential and core competencies of the young minds by sowing the seeds of intellect and human values into them using practices and pedagogy comparable to the best available in the world.
