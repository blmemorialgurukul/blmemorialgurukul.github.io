Recent Events

At BLMG, we host countless events throughout the school year that help stimulate kids and bring the best out of them. Click here to read more about what programs and opportunities we have for students

Salad Party

We took a day to teach students to importance of a balanced, nutritous diet. Eating vegetables and fruits regularly will help keep you healthy!

Earth Day

Our younger class students took a day to celebrate Earth Day and learn about how important nature. Everyone drew drawings and made artwork depicting nature, and each student brought home with them a plant that they could grow in their own homes.

Independence Day

On August 15, we celebrated India's Independance and held a parade around our campus for both students and parents. Our students prepared hard for this event, both decorating the school and practicing dances and speeches.
