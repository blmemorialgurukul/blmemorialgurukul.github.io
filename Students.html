A student focused environment

Here at BLMG we strive to develop an environment where all students can learn and succeed. Specializing in nursery through 10th standard education, we understand that every student learns at their own speed, and we do our best to accomodate each and every individual in their journey for knowledge.

How do you help weaker students who may be struggling with school?

Everyone is welcome here at BLMG. Often times students can struggle with certain topics and that is ok, we make sure that these students are provided the time and help needed to succeeed. Extra classes are offered after school that anyone can attend to understand course content more and prepare for tests. The parents of these students will be regularly informed on their child's performance.

When is school held?

Summer term: 8:30AM to 1:45PM

Winter term: 9:00AM to 2:15PM

How can I stay  involved in my child's education?

We believe that education is a joint responsibility between teachers and parents. At regular intervals, we will have open hous meetings where you can meet with your child's teachers and talk about their education.
